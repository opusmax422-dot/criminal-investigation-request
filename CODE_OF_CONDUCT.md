# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

This repository contains formal legal documentation of an active criminal investigation. All interactions must maintain the highest standards of professionalism and respect for the serious nature of the subject matter.

## Expected Behavior

### For Contributors
- Maintain professional and respectful communication
- Focus on technical improvements that enhance documentation quality
- Respect the factual and legal nature of the content
- Report security vulnerabilities responsibly
- Provide constructive feedback on technical aspects

### For Viewers/Users
- Treat the documented evidence and claims with appropriate seriousness
- Respect the legal process and those involved
- Use the information responsibly
- Report any technical issues or broken links

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are not tolerated:
- Harassment, intimidation, or disrespectful conduct
- Attempts to obscure, alter, or misrepresent evidence
- Frivolous or bad-faith contributions
- Disclosure of sensitive personal information
- Interference with legal processes
- Spam or off-topic content

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:
- All repository interactions (issues, pull requests, discussions)
- Communications related to this project
- Representation of this project in public spaces

## Enforcement

### Reporting

Violations can be reported to:
- **Email**: flongo11@gmail.com
- **Subject**: "Code of Conduct Violation"

### Consequences

Violations may result in:
1. **Warning**: First-time minor violations
2. **Temporary Ban**: Repeated or moderate violations
3. **Permanent Ban**: Severe or persistent violations
4. **Legal Action**: For serious violations involving evidence tampering or harassment

## Context

This repository documents serious criminal allegations supported by evidence. The gravity of these matters requires:
- Sensitivity to victims and those affected
- Respect for legal processes
- Commitment to truth and transparency
- Professional conduct at all times

## Attribution

This Code of Conduct is adapted for the specific context of legal documentation and criminal investigation materials.
